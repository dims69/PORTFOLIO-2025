Dark mode website for a dental prosthetics lab near Lyon. I also built a tool that lets dentists upload 3D scan files straight to the lab's server. No more emails, no more USB sticks.

Client Laboratoire 32
Year 2025
Role Design & Dev
Type Website & Business Tool

The Context

Laboratoire 32 makes dental prosthetics near Lyon, mixing traditional handwork with digital tools (CAD/CAM). They had no website at all and needed one that actually reflected the quality of what they do.

The Challenge

Two things to figure out. First, build a visual identity around their existing logo. Second, find a way for dentists to send 3D scan files without relying on email attachments or USB drives.

Goals

  • Build a "Dark & Gold" brand system from the logo colors
  • Add visual effects (WebGL particles via Vanta.js)
  • Automate 3D digital impression intake
  • Connect the site to the lab's local server (NAS)

My approach

Dark background, gold accents. The particle animation (Vanta.js) adds a technical feel without getting in the way. On the practical side, I built a "Practitioner Portal" where a dentist uploads a file and it lands directly on the lab's NAS. The whole pipeline is automated.

Results

Brand Image

Dark blue and gold instead of the usual white medical look. The lab stands out the second you land on the site.

WebGL Effects

Vanta.js particles in the background and stacking cards on scroll. It feels technical without slowing things down or getting in the way of the content.

Productivity

Form, API, OneDrive, NAS. The whole chain is automated. 3D files hit the lab's production server without anyone touching them.
