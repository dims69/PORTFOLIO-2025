Best-Of DeWalt

Layout Print / Editorial InDesign

Layout for the DeWalt "Best Of" catalogues, ranging from 36 to 64 pages per edition. Three releases a year, pixel-perfect international brand guidelines, and hundreds of product references to manage.

Client DeWalt
Year 2024
Role Print Designer
Deliverables Catalogues & POS

The Context

The "Best Of" is DeWalt's flagship catalogue in France. It comes out three times a year, and each edition takes several weeks to produce. It's a high-volume project: hundreds of technical references, visuals to create, and tight deadlines to hit.

The Challenge

Producing these catalogues three times a year takes serious organization. The challenge is keeping up with the intense production rhythm while refreshing the design for each edition, without ever making a mistake on the many technical references.

Key Challenges

  • Keep the pace (3 catalogues / year, up to 64 pages)
  • Coordinate with the creative team and the client
  • Refresh the layout for every edition
  • Zero errors on references and prices

My Role

I created product visuals and laid out a portion of the catalogue in InDesign. I was also involved in client management: progress reviews, approvals, adjustments. Handling both sides in parallel cut down on back-and-forth and helped us deliver on time.

Results

Technical Precision

Up to 64 pages per edition with hundreds of product references. I used InDesign automation features to keep the pace without sacrificing quality.

International Guidelines

DeWalt has strict brand guidelines, identical across all countries. Every visual, every typeface, every color must align with the global standards.

Teamwork

Multi-designer production with deadlines tied to retail distribution cycles. Strong teamwork and solid organization were essential.
