Ford Focus Wrap

Graphic Design Illustrator Branding

Full vehicle wrap for a 2018 Ford Focus for CAP INFO. Geometric patterns, red and black contrast, and vector files aligned to the millimeter for installation.

Client CAP INFO
Role Print Designer
Tools Illustrator, Photoshop
Vehicle Ford Focus 2018

The Context

CAP INFO specializes in retail solutions. Their old vehicle wrap looked outdated and no longer matched the image they wanted to project. They were after something more modern, with a look that turns heads on the road.

The Challenge

The design has to follow the car's body lines: door handles, bumpers, wheel arches. Every graphic element must adapt to the vehicle's shape while staying readable and consistent with CAP INFO's red and black brand.

Goals

  • Give the company car a more current look
  • Adapt the design to the Ford Focus curves and contours
  • Use geometric patterns to break away from the generic fleet look
  • Deliver print-ready vector files

My Approach

I tested several directions in Illustrator before going with a motorsport-inspired style. Geometric shapes in flat red and black that follow the body lines. The contrast is strong, the car stands out from a distance. That was the whole point.

Results

Visibility

Red and black on white is hard to miss. The car became a real mobile ad that identifies CAP INFO at a glance, even from a distance.

Technical Precision

Every file was prepared with proper bleeds and vector paths. Patterns follow the curves, handles and bumpers with no misalignment during installation.

Modernized Image

We went from a plain fleet marking to a motorsport-inspired look. The car gives the company a more current, dynamic image.
